# Why Skupio

The decisions behind how Skupio is built. The same content lives at **[skupio.co/why-skupio](https://skupio.co/why-skupio)** in marketing-shaped form; this is the docs-shaped version for operators and evaluators who want the longer story.

## Browser-first, by architecture

Skupio's LinkedIn touch happens inside your own browser session, through the Chrome extension. There is no cloud-hosted LinkedIn session in our datacentres. There are no third-party IPs touching LinkedIn on your behalf.

This matters in three ways:

- **Account-flag risk.** A cloud tool that signs into LinkedIn from a datacentre IP looks, to LinkedIn, like a bot. A browser extension running in your own session looks, to LinkedIn, like you. The legal and technical posture is the same as any compliant LinkedIn plugin.
- **Data flow.** Your LinkedIn cookies, your DM contents, your connection graph — none of these are transmitted to or stored on our servers. We see only the leads you choose to push into a campaign.
- **Exit ramp.** If Skupio's API ever goes offline, the extension still works for collection and CSV export. Your pipeline does not depend on our uptime to keep your data.

## End-to-end, in one workflow

Most outbound stacks chain a scraper, an enricher, a verifier, an AI writer, a sender, an inbox classifier, and an analytics dashboard. Each tool gets a separate bill, a separate quota, a separate quirk to debug. The hand-offs are where leads disappear.

Skupio runs **[the 7-stage workflow](/concepts/the-7-stage-workflow)** end-to-end so the hand-offs are internal:

- One credentials surface.
- One quota to track.
- One bill.
- One operator workflow.

Your only external dependency is Instantly for the actual sending, because your sending infrastructure should stay under your control.

## EU-hosted by default

Skupio's API, dashboard, queue layer, and storage run in **Frankfurt, EU**. There is no US-primary infrastructure for customer data. For agencies with EU data residency requirements, the Agency tier supports workspace-level data residency commitments via DPA.

## Privacy footprint

The blast radius of a worst-case breach matters. Skupio's footprint is small by design:

- **What we store:** workspace metadata, your scraped lead lists, your sequence drafts, your campaign analytics, your reply logs.
- **What we don't store:** your LinkedIn session cookies, your LinkedIn DM bodies, your connection graph, your sending mailbox passwords.

If we were ever breached, what's at risk is your campaign work — not your LinkedIn identity and not your sender infrastructure. That distinction is deliberate.

## Operator-controlled retention

- **Default retention** for raw scraped lead data: 90 days, then automatic deletion.
- **Default retention** for leads you've actioned (added to a campaign): indefinite, but you can delete any time.
- **Workspace owner** can shorten either default.
- **Self-serve right-to-erasure**: delete a workspace from the dashboard; the cascade clears every scraped list, every sequence draft, every reply log within 7 days.

## Tiered, published pricing

Plans and limits are visible up front in **[Plans and limits](/reference/plans-and-limits)**. No "contact sales for pricing" gates on the core product. No agency tax, no per-seat surprises.

The pricing reveals at v3.0 launch with Stripe checkout. Until then, beta users have grandfathered limits.

## Audio-first creative iteration

For the three studios (Offer, Audience, Sequence), you can record a 60-second voice memo and Claude Opus drafts the artifact. Voice is faster than the keyboard for creative iteration: you re-record the part that needs to change rather than retyping the whole thing.

This is the single biggest change in how Skupio feels compared to most outreach tools. The AI listens to you, not the other way around.

## What we won't do

- We will not run a cloud LinkedIn session on a customer's behalf. The browser extension is the architecture, full stop.
- We will not mirror your DMs or store your inbox contents in our cloud.
- We will not replicate your connection graph into a database we control.
- We will not sell, resell, or repackage your lead data.
- We will not gate the core workflow behind "contact sales" pricing.

## Compliance posture

- **GDPR processor relationship**: Skupio is a data processor for the leads you upload into a workspace. Everything else (your LinkedIn session, your DMs, your network) stays in your browser.
- **DPA available** on request before paid plans activate. Standard SCCs for any non-EU sub-processor we use.
- **LinkedIn ToS**: we run as a browser extension in your own LinkedIn session — the same legal posture as any compliant LinkedIn plugin.
