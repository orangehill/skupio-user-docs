## What each plan is for

- **Free** — try the loop end-to-end on a small list. Run a real campaign with 50 leads, see what the AI writes, learn the reply triage.
- **Starter** — solo founders and small teams running one or two campaigns at a time. Enough quota to keep a consistent week-on-week outreach cadence.
- **Pro** — teams running multiple parallel campaigns, multi-mailbox sending, and serious volume. Most paid customers land here.
- **Agency** — multi-workspace billing, self-host option on contract, dedicated support, and the volume limits agencies need across client accounts.

## Beta-tier details

All Skupio v3.0 beta users have access to the **Pro** feature set with the **Free** quota limits, plus 1,000 extra enrichment credits per month for the first 90 days. At pricing launch, beta users choose their plan and keep their grandfathered limits for 12 months.

If you signed up before pricing reveals and need higher quotas for testing, **[email us](mailto:hi@skupio.co?subject=Beta%20quota)** — we accommodate most reasonable requests.

## Self-host

Self-hosting is available **only on the Agency tier under enterprise contract**, with workspace-level data residency commitments. **[Email us](mailto:hi@skupio.co?subject=Self-host)** to start a conversation about scope and pricing.

## What's NOT in the table

Quotas you don't have to think about on any plan:

- Number of lead lists per workspace (unlimited)
- Number of sequence drafts per workspace (unlimited)
- Number of A/B variants per step (up to 4 active)
- Inbox retention (replies kept for 24 months active, archive on request)
